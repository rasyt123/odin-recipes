Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

Elevate ramen noodles with melted cheese to create a creamy, comfort-food meal in minutes. Home cooks enjoy customizing this recipe with eggs, veggies, or extra cheese for fresh flavors. Allrecipes member Geri says, "I make it all the time, but also add a poached egg! It is my favorite!"

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
